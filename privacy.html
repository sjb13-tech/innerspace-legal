Privacy Policy

InnerSpace: Sleep & Sound
Last updated: May 27, 2026

Information We Collect

InnerSpace does not collect personally identifiable information. The app stores the following data locally on your device only: session history, mood check-ins, streaks, and app preferences.

Purchases & Subscriptions

InnerSpace offers auto-renewable subscriptions processed through Apple In-App Purchases. Purchase and billing information is handled entirely by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment details.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat – subscription management (stores an anonymous user ID)
  • Supabase – audio content delivery (CDN only, no personal data)
  • Expo / EAS – app delivery and crash reporting

Audio Content

All audio content is streamed from our secure CDN. No audio is recorded from your device.

Children's Privacy

InnerSpace is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
