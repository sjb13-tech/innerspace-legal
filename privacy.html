Privacy Policy

InnerSpace: Sleep & Sound  ·  Last updated: May 27, 2026

InnerSpace ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user of the InnerSpace app.

1. Information We Collect

InnerSpace does not require you to create an account. We collect minimal data to provide a personalised experience.

Stored locally on your device only:

  • Mood check-ins and emotional history
  • Daily streaks and usage stats
  • Sound Temple mix preferences
  • Onboarding and app preferences
  • AI journal session count (to manage usage limits)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

InnerSpace does not access, collect, or store your location data at any time.

2. AI Journal Feature

InnerSpace includes an AI-powered journal that generates reflective responses to your thoughts. When you use this feature:

  • Your messages are sent to Anthropic (makers of Claude AI) via a secure Supabase Edge Function
  • Messages are processed in real time to generate a response and are not stored on our servers beyond the active session
  • Anthropic may process this data in accordance with their own privacy policy

See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

We recommend you do not include sensitive personal information such as financial details, passwords, or identifying information about others in your journal entries.

3. Subscriptions & Purchases

InnerSpace offers auto-renewable subscriptions processed through Apple In-App Purchases:

  • Monthly: $9.99 per month
  • Annual: $59.99 per year

Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your Apple ID Account Settings.

Subscription management and receipt validation is handled by RevenueCat, which stores an anonymous user identifier and purchase history to track entitlements. We do not store your payment details.

4. Audio Content

All audio content (sessions, affirmations, Sound Temple layers) is streamed from our secure CDN hosted on Supabase. No audio is recorded from your device at any time. Your IP address may be processed by Supabase's CDN infrastructure solely for the purpose of delivering content — it is not stored or used for any other purpose.

5. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat – subscription and entitlement management. Collects anonymous user ID, device type, app version, and purchase history. Privacy Policy
  • Supabase – audio CDN and secure backend functions. Processes IP addresses for content delivery only. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) – AI journal responses. Journal messages are processed in real time and not retained. Privacy Policy
  • Expo / EAS – app delivery, over-the-air updates, and crash reporting. May collect device information and crash logs. Privacy Policy
  • Apple – In-App Purchases, push notifications, and App Store distribution. Privacy Policy

Some of these services are based in the United States. By using InnerSpace, you consent to your data being processed in countries outside your own where data protection laws may differ.

6. Push Notifications

InnerSpace may send optional push notifications (e.g. daily reminders, streak nudges). You can enable or disable these at any time in your device Settings. We do not use notification data for advertising purposes.

7. Data Retention

All locally stored data (mood history, streaks, preferences) remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. We do not retain your personal data on our servers.

8. Security

All data transmitted between the app and our backend services is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Locally stored data is protected by your device's built-in security. While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorised access or disclosure.

9. Children's Privacy

InnerSpace is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

10. Your Rights

Since InnerSpace does not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no account data to access, correct, or delete. To remove all locally stored data, simply delete the app from your device.

If you have used the AI journal feature and have concerns about data processed by Anthropic, please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy or contact them directly.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you may have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via an in-app notice or App Store update notes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
